I have worked on projects encompassing both experimental and computational quantum hardware research. Here is a cumulative list of these projects.

Towards Andreev Qubits

This was my Master's Thesis project where I fabricated 15 samples and three different types of device designs with Bilayer Graphene Quantum Dots and Superconductors.
